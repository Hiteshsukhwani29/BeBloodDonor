Eligibility
1) Your weight should be 45kg or above.
2) Minimum haemoglobin count should be 12.5 gm.
3) Pulse rate should be between 50 and 100 per minute.
4) Body temperature should be normal and oral temperature should not exceed 37.5 degree Celsius.
5) Should not have donated blood within last three months.
6) Should not have been treated malaria in last three.
7) Should not have consumed alcohol within last 9 hours.
